Wrestling Youtubers

So this is my one "socially-outcast" vice: pro wrestling. I mark out anytime I see a great match or a quality promo. I love the art behind it. And in that vein, I enjoy listening to people talk the art of pro wrestling. From Brian Zane and Wrestling With Wregret to Oli Davis and Luke Owen at , and from the guys at Cultaholic to various other spots, their analysis and humor makes even the most mundane chores more enjoyable. Podcasts are great, and these are even better.
